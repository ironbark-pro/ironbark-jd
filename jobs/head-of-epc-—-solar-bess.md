**Description**

**Key Responsibilities (not limited to):**

* Own EPC strategy and execution across the portfolio; set technical and commercial standards.
* Select and manage EPC contractors; negotiate contracts and oversee contractor performance and HSE compliance.
* Coordinate handover from development to execution; work with finance, legal and development teams.

* Ensure compliance with German and EU technical, environmental and HSE standards.
* Support risk mitigation, cost control and delivery governance.

**Requirements:**

* 8+ years in renewable project delivery with focus on solar and/or BESS.
* Demonstrated track record delivering large-scale projects in Germany/EU.
* Technical degree in engineering; PMP or similar is a plus.
* Fluent German preferred; comfortable with EU travel.
