 Senior on-site lead accountable for end-to-end delivery of a fully integrated build for mission-critical projects (datacentres, life sciences, hyperscale). Reports to BU Management and Operations Director. Sets direction for safe, on-time, on-budget delivery and leads the core project management team.

**Key Responsibilities (not limited to):**

* Lead project team and client interface; chair weekly team/design/client meetings and follow up on actions.
* Implement and drive project audit plan; ensure closure of audit findings and continual improvement.
* Own EHS, quality and compliance on the project; attend and contribute to EHS/QA and shift planning meetings.
* Manage procurement planning, engineering development and subcontractor coordination to meet milestones.
* Track progress vs plan; produce weekly reports, lookaheads and planner updates; implement recovery strategies where needed.
* Control budgets and margins; lead regular commercial reviews and manage claims/variations.
* Mentor and train the project team; define training standards for staff and subcontractors.
* Maintain strong client relations and represent the company at client meetings.


**Requirements:**

* Engineering degree or recognized trade qualification.
* 10+ years’ experience managing large construction projects as a general contractor.
* Thorough knowledge of civil, structural and architectural packages.
* Proven ability to lead teams, set expectations and drive performance.
* Strong communication and IT literacy.
* Attention to detail and strong team-working skills.


**Conditions:**

* Project-based benefits and professional development; flexible working patterns where required
