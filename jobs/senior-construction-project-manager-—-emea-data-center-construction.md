**Description**
 Hands-on construction manager acting as the client’s on-site representative on data-centre builds. Responsible for contractor oversight, safety leadership, quality control and commercial administration of construction contracts.

**Key Responsibilities (not limited to):**

* Act as Company’s site representative; manage general contractors across civil, structural, electrical, mechanical and commissioning disciplines.
* Enforce safety-first culture; identify and escalate observations and corrective actions.
* Administer construction contracts and commercial elements, including bids, change orders and claims.
* Inspect works, support commissioning, review submittals and ensure compliance with contract documents.
* Produce schedule, cost and budget reports; forecast and manage commercial risk.
* Support procurement reviews and contractor evaluations; lead resolution of claims and major variations.
* Mentor junior team members and collaborate across cross-functional teams.

**Requirements:**

* 7+ years recent construction management on large MEP/MEP-heavy projects (data centres or industrial MEP plants).
* Experience with vendor and general contractor management; strong commercial acumen.
* Preferred: 10+ years site management, professional certification (CCM / AC / CPC) and degree in engineering or construction management.
* Ability to travel and work across EMEA.
